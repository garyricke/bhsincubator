About the Program

Real Startups. Real Mentors. Real Funding.

INCubator@BHS is a nationally recognized entrepreneurship program at Batavia High School, part of the Uncharted Learning network of ~90 schools. Students form actual startups, develop real products using the Lean Startup methodology, and pitch to panels of industry judges — all within a single school year.

“The INCubator@BHS program offers BHS students an authentic entrepreneurship experience. Students are able to develop, test, and modify their own product or service under the mentoring of experienced industry professionals.”

— Brian Gamlin, Director, INCubatoredu@BHS
